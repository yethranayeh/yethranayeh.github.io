About

I am a 22 year old English Language and Literature student interested in software development. I have worked on front-end projects as well as Shopify Theme development.

I have experience in Python, with intermediate knowledge of web-scraping, Django and database management applications (PostgreSQL, SQLite, MongoDB).

Projects

Below is a collection of my projects that I have worked on.

Daily Mood Rater

Daily Mood Rater is a desktop app where you can rate your mood daily from 1 to 10 and keep track of your mood history by month with visual bar graphs.

EtsyScraper

A Django web application that can scrape Product Name, Price, Image Link from Etsy product links. The scraped data is then saved into a PostgreSQL database.
